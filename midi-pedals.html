INTERNAL PULL-UP RESISTORS

When we set the voltage high a la the lines of code we're using what's called a pull-up resistor to set the voltage. There's a bunch of little resistors inside the Arduino, and we're just telling the Arduino to route the voltage through there

So what's R2? You can think of that as a representation of all the Other Stuff in the Arduino. There's a ton in there: LEDs, capacitors, resistors, transistors, diodes, you name it. But we're a pretty advanced society, and we've figured out how to measure the impedance is 'looking into' the Arduino. So we know we can pretend there's a 10MΩ resistor there.
